The Flamboyance of Flamingos

To be honest, we thought that a group of Flamingos were called a “flock”. Considering the fact that these beautiful birds are pink and not to mention “Flamboyance” is too cool of a word to ignore, the team has decided to use this to show solidarity and support for Breast Cancer victims. We have also chosen different shades of pink as agent pseudonyms on our mission to raise awareness for Breast Cancer.
